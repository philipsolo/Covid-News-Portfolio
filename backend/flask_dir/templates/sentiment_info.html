Natural Language Analysis

What/How?


The sentiment scrore ranges between -1.0 (negative) and 1.0 (positive). It corresponds to the overall emotional leaning of the text, furthermore, magnitude indicates the overall strength of emotion (both positive and negative) within the given text, between 0.0 and +inf. 


Unlike score, magnitude is not normalized; each expression of emotion within the text (both positive and negative) contributes to the text's magnitude (so longer text blocks may have greater magnitudes). Magnitude is the balance between two opposing forces negative-positive, usually in my experience unbiased articles have two attributes a sentiment value of 0 and a magnitude ranging from 10-60         

Article Analysis


After scrapping and analyzing multiple articles a pattern  emerges between the average news sentiment/magnitude of an article and  its bias. Specifically there is a correlation between how sentimental an article is (negatively or positively) and how biased it is.In order to collect this data as  subjectively and scientifically as possible the news sites are scanned every 5 min for weeks. Unfortunately not all news sources  provide articles daily, but weekly therefore skewing the data (as some days can have tragic events taking place), fortunately this problem can be averted in the long term collection of data as a better daily average can be conceived.
